WELCOME TO
THE YOUTHS INTEREST PLATFORM

Youths Interest 2027 is not just a focus - it is a movement, a vision, and a platform, designed to empower young people, drive entrepreneurship, prepare them for leadership opportunities, while also promoting inclusive development across generations. Today, many of our youths are talented, hardworking, and intelligent—but what they lack is opportunity, support, and direction. That is why this initiative focuses on practical solutions, not just promises.

THE LEADER:

Comr. Akanimo Udiok

Meet the leader of the Youth Interest Movement Akanimoh Udiok a result-driven marketing professional and business administrator with extensive experience in sales, brand development, and market expansion. He hails from Uruan Local Government Area in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. in his words,
"The time has come for our youths to move from the background to the forefront of development. With your support, your guidance, and your partnership, Youths Interest 2027 will become a platform that transforms lives and strengthens our community. Let us work together. Let us believe in our youths. Let us build the future—starting today."
