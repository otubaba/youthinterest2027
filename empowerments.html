Motorcycle Riders Program

On 13th March 2026, Akanimoh Udiok embarked on a remarkable empowerment initiative aimed at supporting local motorcycle riders. Recognizing the challenges faced by riders due to rising fuel costs and economic hardship, he took the extraordinary step of fueling over 100 motorcycles. This act was not just generosity—it was a strategic move to alleviate the daily burdens of hardworking riders who form a crucial part of the community's transport and logistics network. By directly providing fuel, Akanimoh demonstrated care, solidarity, and a commitment to uplifting the local workforce, setting an inspiring example of how individuals can make a tangible difference in the lives of others. The initiative received widespread appreciation, highlighting Akanimoh's dedication to practical empowerment, community support, and the well-being of everyday heroes who keep society moving.
